LIFESCAPE HOTEL ROOMS

Your serene escape where comfort, elegance, and nature come together to create unforgettable stays.

SINGLE ROOM

₱1,650 per night

Single room offers comfort and privacy, featuring a cozy bed with a bedside table, a personal closet for storage, and a 40-inch television for your entertainment. The room includes its own private restroom for convenience. Selected rooms provide scenic views—some overlook the serene beach, while others offer picturesque sights of the lush garden and pool area.

DELUXE ROOM

₱4,500 per night

The Deluxe Room features a private balcony with a stunning beach view, perfect for relaxing and soaking in the scenery. Inside, it is elegantly furnished with a king-size bed, bedside tables, a coffee table, and a flat-screen TV for your entertainment. A walk-in closet offers generous storage space, while the spacious bathroom includes a bathtub and premium fixtures, providing a luxurious setting to unwind at the end of the day.

CABANA ROOM

₱6,000 per night

The Cabana Room offers a relaxing stay with views of the pool and select rooms featuring beach views. Each unit includes a private balcony, a fridge, a dining table for two, and a 40-inch TV. The interior features natural tones for a calm, coastal feel—perfect for unwinding in comfort.

SUITE ROOM

₱15,000 per night

The luxurious suite boasts stunning views of both the garden and the beach, offering a tranquil and relaxing atmosphere. It includes four private bedrooms, each with its own en-suite restroom for added comfort and privacy. The suite also features a cozy living room, a small kitchen area, and a dining space for convenience and enjoyment.
