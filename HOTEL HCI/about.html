ABOUT US

Lifescape Hotel's goal is to provide a peaceful escape from the stresses of daily life, offering a relaxing environment where guests can unwind and recharge. The hotel aims to create a sanctuary for those seeking a quick getaway, with comfortable accommodations and personalized services that allow guests to disconnect and relax.

With spacious rooms, modern amenities, and wellness services like spa treatments and yoga, Lifescape Hotel is designed to help guests find tranquility and mental clarity. Whether for a brief retreat or a weekend getaway, the hotel offers the perfect setting for relaxation and rejuvenation, allowing guests to return home refreshed and stress-free.

HOTEL POLICY

1. Check-in and Check-Out Policy

  • Check-in time: 2:00 PM
  • Check-out time: 12:00 PM
  • Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

2. Reservation and Cancellation

  • Reservations must be guaranteed with a valid credit card.
  • Cancellations must be made 24 hours in advance.

3. Payment Policy

  • Payment is required at check-in.
  • We accept credit card, bank transfer, or cash.
  • A security deposit may be requested for incidental charges.

4. Bar Policy

  • Bar is open daily from 10:00 AM to 3:00 AM.
  • Guests must be 18+ to order alcoholic beverages.

5. Smoking Policy

  • Smoking is prohibited in all rooms and indoor spaces.

6. Quiet Hours Policy

  • Quiet hours are from 5:00 PM to 7:00 AM.
  • Please minimize noise during these hours.
