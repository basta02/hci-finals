ACCESSIBILITY AT LIFESCAPE

1. Accessible Rooms

  • Our accessible rooms are designed to provide convenience and comfort for guests with mobility challenges. Each room features wider doorways, lower furniture, and accessible bathrooms with grab bars and roll-in showers.

2. Wheelchair Accessibility

  • Our hotel is fully wheelchair accessible, with ramps and elevators available to provide easy access to all floors and common areas. We ensure that every guest can move around our property with ease.

3. Accessible Parking

  • Convenient, designated parking spots for guests with disabilities are available near the hotel entrance, providing direct access to the building.

4. Assistance Services

  • If you require additional assistance during your stay, our team is happy to accommodate. From luggage handling to arranging accessible transportation, we’re here to make your stay comfortable.

5. Hearing and Visual Impairments

  • We provide rooms equipped with visual alarm systems for the hearing impaired and offer Braille signage throughout the hotel to support guests with visual impairments.

6. Service Animals

  • Service animals are always welcome at our hotel, and we ensure that your furry companions have everything they need for a comfortable stay.
